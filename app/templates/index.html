What is PrepPulse?

The All-in-One Placement Companion

PrepPulse is an AI-driven platform designed for students preparing for campus placements. It brings together intelligent tools — from AI chatbots and resume analyzers to skill trackers and mock tests — so you can focus on what matters: getting placed.
